About SNAP and SNAPshot Texas

Texans strive every day to make a better life for themselves and their kids.
But with the rising cost of living, it can be hard to make ends meet
— especially after a job loss, illness or accident.

We all need an occational helping hand. The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) helps Texans get through the rough patches by preventing hunger and providing the fuel and nutrition they need to succeed.

SNAP builds stronger families, stronger communities and stronger Texans.
Just like you and me.
